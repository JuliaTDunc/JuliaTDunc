About

Hi, I'm a software engineer from sunny San Diego! I fell in love with coding in 2019, and started bringing my ideas to life, piece by piece. I’m drawn to the blend of creativity and logic, and after years in a different field, now felt like the perfect time to take on something new and challenging in my career. Coding has been just that! Transitioning from customer service to software engineering has been an exciting journey, combining my problem-solving skills with my passion for learning. Outside of work, I’m all about the quiet pleasures— reading, exploring something new, enjoying the outdoors with my dog, Finny. I care deeply about people, art, and how technology can bring us all closer together.
